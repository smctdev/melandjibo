How We Met

Jun 10, 2008

It was the first day of SY 2008-2009 when the couple became classmates during their 5th grade in Elementary. Coincidentally, it was also the birthday of the bride. During this time, they were merely acquaintances. The bride did not even remember that they were classmates, not until the groom mentioned years after.

First Date

Oct 15, 2016

It was not even considered a date by the bride's standards at that time. It was the groom's birthday and simple request to have a lunch out, just the two of them. Right after eating, the bride even went back to school to review for the scholarship exam the next day. Looking back now, the bride considers that moment a lucky charm since she passed the exam which opened a lot of opportunities for her studies and career.

In a Relationship

Jun 01, 2019

Earlier that day, the bride told the groom that she has something to tell him so they should meet up. He proposed to meet at their favorite spot, Jollibee Drive-Thru. It was a simple dinner with their faves and then she started asking him about his plans and making sure he knows what he's been getting into a relationship with. It was a sincere yet somewhat awkward conversation. Then, she told him her first-ever ILoveYou and they became a couple officially. They also met and had a random conversation with a couple from the table next to theirs. It was a simple full of randomness, laughter, and love.

We're Engaged

Jan 14, 2025

There were no extravagant activities and surprises. The bride only requested that the groom ask her hand from her parents which he really did despite him being a very shy type of a person. He courageously planned and faced her parents stating his full intentions to marry their daughter.
